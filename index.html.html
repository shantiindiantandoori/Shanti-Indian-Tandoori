About Us

Located in the beautiful Puerto Rico of Gran Canaria, Shanti Indian Tandoori brings you the richness of Indian flavors in a cozy, welcoming environment. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and traditional spices to craft every dish.

Our Menu

  • Starters: Samosas, Pakoras, Onion Bhajis
  • Mains: Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, Lamb Rogan Josh
  • Tandoori: Chicken Tandoori, Paneer Tikka
  • Vegetarian: Chana Masala, Saag Paneer, Dal Makhani
  • Breads: Naan, Garlic Naan, Cheese Naan
  • Desserts: Gulab Jamun, Mango Kulfi
